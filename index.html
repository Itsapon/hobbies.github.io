At the start of the pandemic I took up knitting as a creative hobby to pass the time and spend my days in lockdown learning a new useful skill. Since then I'm all wrapped up in scarfs, sweaters, socks and everything comfy!
Sometimes inspiration strikes you and you have to act on it; sometimes what you need to do is just get off screen and work with your hands. Wrapping stones with leather, resin casting with a friend... you name it!
As a little kid drawing used to be my passion, and while over the years life has gotten in the way of constant drawing, it is still a favourite pasttime of mine. From graphite to watercolour to acrylics, there's just something magical about seeing something come to life in front of you.
